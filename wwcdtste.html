Integrate it into our daily lives

We all have a part in saving the earth. One thing we can do individually is integrate saving the earth into each our daily lives by cultivating habits to do so which can be implemented in our everyday activities. For example, you can filter recyclable objects from normal waste. After finishing milk or fruit juice cartons, drinks in plastic bottles or cans, or after using rough paper. Instead of just disposing them into the waste bin, you can put them into a separate plastic bag or small bin and throw them into a recycle bin near your house. Thus, you'll be contributing to saving the earth everyday by contributing objects to be reused and recycled rather than just wasting them by simply disposing them as normal trash. Other saving the earth habits you can cultivate into your daily activities include half-flushing the toilet after use, brushing your teeth from a mug and recyle old documents as rough paper or printing paper. All in all, with small habits, we all can make a big change to the earth and create a better and sustainable future for ourselves as long as we all do our part and integrate some of these activities into our everyday lives .

Goals that you can set in doing your part in sustainable development


As mentioned before, we all have a part to contribute in creating a better and sustainable future.
I believe that one can contribute more by focusing on 1 part of environment sustainability. Below are some goals you can have and focus on in order to contribute to achieving a sustainable future

1. Reduce Energy Consumption


One of the simplest ways to promote sustainable development is to reduce energy consumption. This can be achieved by turning off lights and electronic devices when not in use, using energy-efficient light bulbs, and using natural light instead of artificial light where possible.

2. Reduce Water Usage


Conserving water is another effective way to promote sustainable development. Individuals can set goals to reduce their water consumption by fixing leaks, using water-efficient appliances, and taking shorter showers.

3. Reduce Waste Generation


Reducing waste generation is essential for promoting sustainable development. Individuals can set goals to reduce their waste by recycling, composting, and reducing the use of disposable products like plastic bags, straws, and water bottles. They can also opt for products that are made from sustainable materials and packaged in eco-friendly materials.
